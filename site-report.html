My Experience

Web development has always been fun and challenging to learn. Learning basics of web development has been incredable journey. The concept of designing and debugging process has been tough in early few days. But practice and available online resources helped to debug. I am still improving and expect this learning and implementing our learning will be very fun journey.

The implementation of hamburger menu in small devices was very much tough for me. It costs me more than a day to figure out the working solution. And the best part of implementing hambuger menu was it gave me alot of confidence and motivation to move forward to find solution for other problems as well.

The UI designed by me is simple containing header section with nav menu, footer section with copyright text and the main section for actual content. The design is on dark theme with white text. The UI consits hover effects on buttons, navlinks etc. Active navlinks have brownish orange color which gives glow to our dark theme.
